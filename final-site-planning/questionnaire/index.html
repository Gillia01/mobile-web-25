Website Planning Questionnaire

Planning Questions

Who is the target audience?

People who are in need for shoes.

How can I tailor the website to reach that audience?

I will use a clean, modern, and mobile friendly desing. I will use filters for brands, size, price, and styles. Also offer deals, promotions and litited-time releases.

What are the goals for the site?

To provide an easy- to- use shopping experience with secure checkout, Increase website traffic and engagement.

How will I gather the information?

Research other competitor websites like Nike, Adidas, and Foot Locker. Collect product details from suppliers and manufacturers.

What are my sources for multimedia content?

Official product images and videos from shoe manufacturers. High-resolution stock photos.

What is my budget?

Hosting and domain: $50-$100/year. Website development: $0 Marketing and ads: $ 100- $500/ Month inventory and supplier costs may vary.

How long do I have to complete the project?

1 month

Who is on my project team?

Myself (Developer and Designer)

How often should the site be updated?

Product listings and inventory updates: daily or weekly Security and feature updates: Monthly

Who is responsible for updating the site?

To start, Myself will handle all updates. As the site grows, I may hire a content manger or web admin.
