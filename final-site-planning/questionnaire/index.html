Website Planning Questionnaire

Planning Questions

Who is the target audience?

People wanting to purchase shoes.

How can I tailor the website to reach that audience?

Use a cozy, inviting, and modern design, Mobile-first responsive layout

What are the goals for the site?

Attract more foot traffic to the website.

How will I gather the information?

Communicate with website owner, review local competitors websites and social media

What are my sources for multimedia content?

High-resolution images of shoe.

What is my budget?

Hosting and domain: $50-$100/year. Website development: $0 Marketing and ads: $ 50-$200/ Month Photography: $0-$100 (DIY or student photographer)

How long do I have to complete the project?

5 weeks

Who is on my project team?

Myself (Developer and Designer)

How often should the site be updated?

Menu and promotions: Weekly Events page: Monthly or as needed Blog and customer stories: Bi-monthly Security and plugin updates: Monthly

Who is responsible for updating the site?

To start, Myself will handle all updates. As the site grows, I may hire a content manger or web admin.
